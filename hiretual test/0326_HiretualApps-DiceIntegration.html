How does Dice work with Hiretual?

The Dice-Hiretual integration combines rich active candidate Dice data with Hiretual’s massive passive candidate data and it’s AI Sourcing capabilities to create the super tool to source and pipeline your tech-related projects. For positions that need immediate filling, the Dice database gives you immediate access to 2 million resumes for tech professionals. The seamless integration between the two platforms allows you to use the same workflow on Hiretual to source both active and passive candidates in one place.
